Quantum Leap

Welcome to Quantum Leap!

This is a puzzle-platformer based on the quantum principle of superposition. In the quantum world, a particle can exist in multiple places at once until it's observed. Use this ability to your advantage!

How to Play: Use the arrow keys to move and jump. Press 'S' to enter superposition, which will show you possible future locations. Click on one of the 'ghosts' to collapse your position and teleport there. Can you navigate the void?
